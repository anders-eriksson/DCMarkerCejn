TypeOfMachine is used for selecting the specific machine that the program should run on.

TypeOfMachine		Machine
1					ABB Machine
2					Automatisk (samma som ABB)
3					Ultraflow
4					Nippelflöde - Kopplingsflöde
5					Flexibel 1 - Flexibel 2
6					Stor Flexibel 1 - Stor Flexibel 2
7					208 - 210
